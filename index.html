GroupDīn Description

GroupDīn was made for group planners. You know, the one in your group of friends whose job it is to pick a restaurant and make reservations, otherwise you'd end up staying in and ordering pizza...again. Every group's got one. It allows those individuals to create a list of restaurants with descriptions and send out the options seemlessly to the rest of the group. Try it out!
