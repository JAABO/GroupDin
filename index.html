GroupDīn Description

GroupDīn was made for group planners. You know, the one in your group of friends whose job it is to pick a restaurant and make reservations, otherwise you'd end up staying in and ordering pizza...again. Every group's got one!

GroupDīn allows you to create a list of restaurants and seamlessly send it out to the rest of the group. Try it out! Pick your favorite restaurants or places you've been dying to try and add them below. When you're ready to send out your list, click on the "Make the Plan" button.
